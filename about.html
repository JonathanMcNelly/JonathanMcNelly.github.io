A Freelance History

...that is, just like human existence, a meek example of passage of time. Nonetheless, here are a few people I have worked with in my short life thus far.

Sustainability Connections

This small social enterprise focuses on bringing energy into the hands of ordinary people and tries to defeat fuel-poverty. For this just cause, I have redesigned their website and provided some illustrations. The final website can be viewed Here.

Worldwide Fruit Ltd

In a team of four, I helped to deliver a 3D ethical animation displaying the pitfalls of modern day slavery. I was the storyboard, texture and concept Artist for the project.

Education

University of Kent, 2014-2017, Digital Arts

For the last few years, I have studied Digital Arts at the University of Kent. The course modules included graphic and web design and also featured photography, game design, programming and 3D animation. I also productively used my time there to join Improv theatre. I also learnt to function in a team by completing so many lovely, multi-media group projects, and formed a valueable network of digital creatives. Groupwork isn't so bad, is it?

Court Grammar School, Broadstairs

When I first came to England on an exchange program, I joined this lovely language-focused grammar school and didn't learn any new languages. I did my A-Levels in English Literature, Psychology in Maths and, in the spirit of inconsistency, completed an AS-Level in Art and an AS-Level in Drama! I also did vague things like public speaking and piano practising and improved my English at the cost of forgetting my German.
